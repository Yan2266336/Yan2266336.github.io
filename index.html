I am a postdoctoral researcher at Yale University. My research focuses on Natural Language Processing, Information Extraction, Entity Linking, Biomedical NLP, Financial NLP, and Large Language Models.

My recent work explores benchmark construction, domain-specific information extraction, concept normalization, financial reasoning, and the evaluation of large language models in high-stakes domains.
